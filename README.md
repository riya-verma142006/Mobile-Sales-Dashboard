# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across different cities, brands, payment methods, customer ratings, and time periods.

This dashboard provides valuable business insights that help stakeholders monitor sales trends, customer behavior, and overall business performance.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The Mobile Sales Dashboard enables users to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze sales by mobile brand and model
- Track monthly sales quantity trends
- Compare transactions across payment methods
- Evaluate customer rating distributions
- Analyze city-wise sales performance
- Filter data dynamically using slicers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Cards
The dashboard displays key business metrics:

- Total Sales: **167M**

- Total Transactions: **792**

### 🔹 Interactive Filters

Users can filter the dashboard using:

- Mobile Model
- Brand
- Payment Method
- Month Selection

---

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

#### 📍 Total Sales by City
- Geographical visualization of sales performance across cities.
- Helps identify high-performing regions.

#### 📅 Total Quantity by Month
- Monthly trend analysis of product sales quantity.
- Identifies seasonal sales patterns.

#### 📱 Total Sales by Mobile Model
- Comparison of revenue generated by different mobile models.

#### 📆 Total Sales by Day Name
- Analysis of sales distribution across weekdays.

---

### 🔹 Customer Insights

#### ⭐ Customer Rating Analysis
Customer ratings are categorized into:

- Good
- Average
- Poor

This helps evaluate customer satisfaction levels.

---

### 🔹 Payment Analysis

#### 💳 Transactions by Payment Method

Comparison of transactions completed through:

- Debit Card
- Credit Card
- UPI

Provides insights into customer payment preferences.

### 🔹 Brand Performance

Sales comparison between major mobile brands including:

Metrics include:
- Total Sales
